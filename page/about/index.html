Hello, my name is Martin

and I'm addicted to climbing

My first attempts at climbing were probably around 2001. Since then I’ve learned a lot, traveled around, met some awesome people and made many new friends.

I particularly enjoy doing long multi-pitch / alpine routes on trad gear, also ice and mixed climbing.

In the end of March 2015 I had a nasty accident - I’ve hit the deck falling from 10 meters and fractured 2 vertebrae. Fortunately, a great deal of luck was involved and I was able to recover very quickly. Month and a half later I’ve spent a week on the rocks of North Wales while taking part in the BMC International Summer Meet which was an absolutely awesome experience.

Climbing is what makes me feel alive, it fuels my desire to learn and grow.

If this all sounds interesting but you’re here to learn about my software development services, you probably want to have a look at my other about page.
